Evidence-Based Therapy, Personalised to You

We use approaches proven by research‚Äîbut therapy isn't one-size-fits-all. Here's how we tailor treatment to your unique needs.

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT)

The gold-standard treatment for anxiety and depression. CBT helps you identify unhelpful thought patterns and develop practical strategies to feel better. It's structured, goal-focused, and backed by decades of research.

Best for: Anxiety, depression, OCD, health anxiety, panic attacks, phobias

Eye Movement Desensitisation and Reprocessing (EMDR)

An evidence-based trauma therapy that helps your brain process distressing memories. EMDR doesn't require you to talk in detail about your trauma‚Äîit works through bilateral stimulation.

Best for: PTSD, complex trauma, flashbacks, intrusive memories

Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)

Rather than fighting difficult thoughts and feelings, ACT helps you accept them while committing to actions aligned with your values. Particularly helpful when life circumstances can't change, but your relationship to them can.

Best for: Chronic pain, long-term health conditions, existential concerns, values-based living

Dialectical Behaviour Therapy (DBT)

Originally developed for borderline personality disorder, DBT teaches skills in mindfulness, distress tolerance, emotion regulation, and interpersonal effectiveness. Highly practical and skills-focused.

Best for: Emotional dysregulation, self-harm, relationship difficulties, intense emotions

Person-Centred Counselling

A non-directive approach that creates space for you to explore your feelings at your own pace. Your therapist provides empathy, unconditional positive regard, and genuine understanding‚Äîwithout judgment or advice-giving.

Best for: Identity exploration, relationship issues, life transitions, general emotional support

Our Services

General Practice Consultations

Comprehensive 30-minute appointments for any health concern. Whether you need diagnosis, treatment, ongoing management, or simply peace of mind, Dr Vale provides expert care with the time you deserve.

Health Screenings & Check-Ups

Executive health assessments, well-person checks, cardiovascular screening, and preventive health reviews. Early detection saves lives—we'll create a screening plan tailored to your age, lifestyle, and risk factors.

Chronic Disease Management

Diabetes, hypertension, asthma, and other long-term conditions require consistent, expert oversight. Our private care ensures you receive regular monitoring, medication reviews, and lifestyle guidance without waiting lists.

Mental Health Support

Initial assessment and management of anxiety, depression, and stress-related conditions. We provide confidential consultations and can refer you to specialist mental health services when appropriate.

Private Referrals & Second Opinions

Fast-track access to Cambridge's leading consultants. We can arrange private specialist appointments within days, not months, and provide expert second opinions on diagnoses or treatment plans.
